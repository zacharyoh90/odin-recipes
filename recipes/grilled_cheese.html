Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description:

This is an easy recipe that requires only 3 ingredients and a pan! Continue reading to see just how easy this delicious comfort food is to make...

Ingredients

  • Bread - 2 slices
  • Cheese - 2 slices
  • Butter - 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. heat pan on medium high heat
  2. while pan is heating, make the sanwich:
    1. lay out slices of bread on cutting board
    2. put two slices of cheese on one of the slices of bread (make sure cheese is evenlt spaced out on bread slice
    3. place the other slice of bread on top of the two-cheese-slice slice of bread
    4. sandwich is ready for cooking!
  3. melt butter in hot pan
  4. add sandwich to hot pan and rub it around in the melted butter to make sure the bottom is evenly coated for frying
  5. let cook for 2-3 mins on each side
  6. if needed, add more butter in between flipping to other side
  7. remove from heat, let cool for 1 minute before slicing in half and eating! Enjoy!
