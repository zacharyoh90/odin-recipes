Scrambled Eggs

Description:

Quick, easy, and delicious scrambled eggs! Just a couple eggs and some standard at home ingredients to make this family pleasing breakfast dish!

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 4
  • American Cheese - 1 slice
  • Milk - 1 tbsp
  • Salt - 1 tsp
  • Pepper - 1 tsp
  • Dried Basil - 1 tsp
  • Butter - 1 tbsp

Steps

  1. heat pan on medium high heat
  2. while pan is heating, scramble eggs and ingredients together:
    1. Get a bowl large enough to fit the 4 cracked eggs with some room
    2. Crack the 4 eggs into the bowl, throwing the shells into the trash
    3. add milk, cheese, salt, pepper, and dried basil to the eggs
    4. whisk together with fork until the yolks are broken and everything is roughly mixed
  3. melt butter in hot pan
  4. pour egg mixture into butter pan
  5. stir immediately with wooden spoon or rubber spatula
  6. let cook for 10 seconds, then stir for 5 seconds
  7. repeat above step until eggs are just solidified and no longer runny
  8. remove from heat and let rest for 1 minute
  9. Enjoy!
