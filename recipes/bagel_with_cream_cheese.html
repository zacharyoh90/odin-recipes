Toasted Bagel with Cream Cheese

Description:

This is an easy recipe that can be completed in minutes with just a few ingredients. Delish!

Ingredients

  • Bagel
  • Cream Cheese - 2 tbsp

Steps

  1. Slice bagel in half, place both halves in the toaster
  2. While bagel is toasting, get cream cheese and butter knife out and standing by
  3. Once bagel is toasted, remove from toaster and spread evenly with 1 tbsp (or more) of cream cheese
  4. Enjoy!
